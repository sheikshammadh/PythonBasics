Bangalore, officially known as Bengaluru, is the capital of the state of Karnataka in India. 
It's the third most populated city in India and is known as the "Silicon Valley of India" because it's a leader in the country's IT sector. 
Here are some things to know about Bangalore:
Location
Bangalore is situated on the Deccan Plateau in the southeast of Karnataka, and is over 900 meters above sea level. 
IT sector
Bangalore is home to the headquarters of many major IT companies, including Infosys, Wipro, Oracle, Google, and Cognizant. 
Educational institutions
Bangalore is home to several educational and research institutions, including the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) 
the Indian Institute of Management (Bangalore) (IIMB), and the National Law School of India University (NLSIU).
